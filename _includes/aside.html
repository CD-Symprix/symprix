Meet the Team

Dr. Sidi Sun

Dr Sidi Sun is the founder of Symprix and a strategic advisor with 14 years of experience in transport and infrastructure. He has worked extensively with governments, operators, and international investors on complex projects involving rail reform, fares strategy, demand forecasting, business case development, and competitive bids.

Sidi delivered high-profile projects for the UK Department for Transport, Rail Baltica, HS2, Hitachi, and East West Rail. His expertise lies in combining rigorous economic and policy analysis with practical, results-driven solutions for clients.

Academically, Sidi holds a PhD in Rail Economics from the University of Leeds, where his research focused on the economic impacts of structural reform in railway systems.

Professional memberships and recognition:

  • Chartered Member of the Chartered Institute of Logistics and Transport (CILT)
  • Overseas Expert for the Chinese Sustainable Transport Innovation Centre (STIC)

Associate Network

Symprix works with a trusted network of senior associates to scale delivery and bring specialist expertise when needed. We have successfully collaborated on bids and advisory engagements. Our associates work across:

  • Strategic transport planning – high-stakes programmes with organisations such as TfL, DfT, and HS2.
  • Modelling & analytics – policy, investment, and operational modelling in the UK and internationally.

This model combines founder-led direction with specialist depth — ensuring agility, credibility, and delivery capacity.
